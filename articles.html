WORLD STRUCTURE

Each Domain in the Death Universe has its own theme.


Most are structured in a circular shape, with the Earthki domain located at the center. However, some worlds may take on unique shapes, while others remain more in a shape of a globe, similar to a typical planet.

Travel between worlds depends on species, technology, or the inherent capabilities of the domain themselves.

Each world has its own system of governance, distinct customs, cultures, and ways of thinking. These differences are largely shaped by the various species that inhabit them.

THE DOMAINS

The Ether: A Domain of light beings, angels, and deities. Powered by multiple suns. Inhabitants: Eths

Earthki: The most "normal" domain, resembling Earth. Earthy beings and animals reside here. Inhabitants: Earthkin

Cosmetica: A domain of stars, weather, and celestial beings. Inhabitants include humanoids formed by/personification of ; the sun or moon, entities born from asteroids and space debris etc
