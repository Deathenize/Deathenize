Summary: "Symbolic powers"


"Symbolic powers/Abilities" are unique abilities linked to characters trough symbols. Some characters are born with them, while others gain them through practice. For example, a flame symbol might grant fire control, or a love symbol might provide healing powers. These symbols unlock and enhance the character's abilities.
