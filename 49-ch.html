Biography

Information

The first-ever human to arrive in the Eldritch realm due to new secret facility tech that was made in the Earthkin realm, Evildra was the first test subject astronaut to travel 100 light-years in space. During her journey, the GPS fell out of control, and she was stuck in space for years before, of course, she embarked on the Eldritch realm, where she then crashed. Evildra is a confident person, but she can sometimes get nervous and can be quite frightened by some of the Eldritch antics, species, and weird activities they engage in.
