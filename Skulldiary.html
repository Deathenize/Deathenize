A revamp on the girl crew thinking of some personality changes and some further new character designs

I've always been intrigued by relationship dynamics. I like things that are very, how could I say, "unconventional," such as making a very masculine character who is a girl, or a very feminine character who is a boy. You'd think that in most relationships depicted in media, there would be some element that counterbalances this, like a masculine character being attracted to a feminine one, or vice versa. While I do enjoy that, I'm also very much into the "Times 2" dynamics: feminine x feminine, masculine x masculine, androgynous, platonic, etc. This also ties into characters that are nonconforming. It's interesting to me and also reflects a part of who I am.
