✦ Biological Overview ✦

Ethereals are often described as “biblically accurate angels” by people from other planets. They live high above the clouds in their own separate world. Their planet is part of a group of planets that rely heavily on solar energy. Ethereals may depend on the sun’s energy the most, as their bodies are made to handle and absorb large amounts of heat.

✦ Anatomy ✦

One key feature of Ethereals is their diamond-shaped hole mark. This mark shows how much sunlight energy they have absorbed. The more broken or worn out it looks, the more energy they’ve taken in. Higher-ranking Ethereals often have fully withered marks because they can absorb more energy than regular Ethereals

✦ Types of Ethereals ✦

1. Winged Citizens: These are the everyday inhabitants of the ethereal realm. They look like typical angels with smaller diamond-shaped holes. Winged citizens live normal lives and often have jobs, like working in the International Post Building, where messages are sent between solar-powered planets. They ussaly also have small wings

2. Guards: Guards protect the entrance to the ethereal realm from invaders. Their like tall soldiers and they usually fight with swords or bows. Some of them also like using their bare hands, though it’s not recommended

3. Workers: Worker’s act like a colony of ants or wasps. They follow strict routines and handle large amounts of paperwork and documents. Each group of workers is led by a head worker who keeps things organized. Workers usually have two wings giant wings making them look unique. If the Etherian leader sends a message, it is processed by these iron 4 arms and workers execute the order.

4. Higher-ups (Iron Four Arms): These angels look closer to “biblically accurate” descriptions, with four eyes and four arms, giving them their title. They act as advisors to the high Etherians and make sure laws are followed.

There are only five Etherians in the ethereal realm at a time, similar to how there are only three High Els in the Eldritch realm.

o Etherians are dressed like emperors and are the most powerful Ethereals.

o Unlike Iron Four Arms, Etherians cannot hide their true form. They need to stay in their natural state to absorb the most sunlight.

✦ Culture ✦

Ethereals wear a lot of golden clothing to reflect their celestial and radiant nature. They often adorn themselves with large golden earrings and intricate jewelry that complements their attire.
When meeting someone of higher status, they customarily sit on their knees as a formal gesture of respect.

Many Ethereals have the tradition of building statues to honor their ancestors, who are held in the highest regard.

Ethereals do not use phones and find them strange. Similarly, they do not have cars; instead, they travel either by flying or on a Pegasi cart through their cities.

In terms of language, Ethereals can communicate in any realm's tongue as long as they wear a bel translator on their ear. Without it, other languages sound like gibberish to them. However, when Ethereals are emotional, their speech becomes less understandable, often shifting into phrases filled with "crosses and holiness." This also applies when they curse, which tends to sound more abstract and sacred.

✦ Fallen Ethereals ✦

Ethereals are considered "fallen" when they are found guilty of treason, betrayal, or criminal acts. Fallen Ethereals are usually banished from the kingdom for eternity. According to legend, the first Ethereal to be banished led to the creation of the planet Limbo. This figure, known as "Dante Inferno," holds a significant place in their mythology/History. Whether its true is up for question for allot of eths

✦ Procreation ✦

Ethereals reproduce sexually, unlike some species such as Fealqins and Eldritches, which can reproduce asexually. That said, Ethereals are exploring lab-based methods to produce stronger soldiers. However, Lunarians view such methods as unethical. This means Ethereals can technically reproduce both sexually and asexually, though sexual reproduction is far more common.

Instead of live births, Ethereal offspring are born from golden eggs, which are carefully placed in bed-like nests. Honeymoons can last up to 500 human days.

It is frowned upon for Ethereals to reproduce with species from planets that are not sun-based. A union between a sun-based and moon-based entity often results in unstable offspring with unpredictable powers. These hybrids, while rare, are typically shunned unless they fulfill an important role. Interestingly, because of their rarity, they are sometimes treated as prized "trophies." (For example, Miracle has one.) Whether these individuals are fond of their situation depends on the specific sun-moon hybrid, often leading to mixed emotions.

✦ Ethical Practices ✦

The International Post Office is a system that benefits all sun-based planets. It facilitates easier communication, seamless message delivery, and smoother trade processes. Thanks to this system, Ethereals and Lunarians living near each other's borders often no longer require translators. Their languages have become so familiar to one another that mutual understanding is possible without additional tools.

Ethereals play a crucial role in maintaining the stability of sun-based planets, protecting them from attacks, and even building other realms with their sun-based powers. These contributions are deeply valued by other realms. Additionally, Ethereal healing power is considered the best across all known realms. Their healing abilities are extraordinarily fast and efficient, making them unparalleled in restorative practices.

✦ Unethical Practices ✦

As with all planetary systems, there are both good and bad practices, and Ethereals are no exception. Despite their calm and friendly demeanor, some of their actions raise ethical concerns.

When dealing with Els (a particular group or entity), Ethereals sometimes try to "unclear" them—a process meant to render them harmless. However, this often results in the Els becoming comatose, numb, or blind, raising serious ethical questions about this practice.

Lunarians also criticize the Ethereals’ attempts to create super soldiers. This practice involves overexerting the sun's energy to produce guards with enhanced abilities, which some view as an irresponsible use of solar power.

Another significant ethical issue is how Ethereals treat mixed sun- and moon-based species. These hybrids are often either banished or treated as rare "trophies," displayed more for their uniqueness than respected for their individuality. This practice has fostered harmful beauty standards for hybrids, treating them like "dog breeds in a fashion show" rather than as sentient beings deserving of equal dignity and respect.
