Age Embodiment

★ How does age operate in skullmare?

- An Indiviual can have an implied age

- An Indiviual can age in human years, earthkins age in human years, and can die of old age

- There are entities that live over a thousand years but still can grow from a child to an adult, its just that time works different in that particular place, and that is shown in their behaviour. Other entities could be described as "ageless" this is a special case when the particular entity is either born as an adult or a child and does not change in their appeareance or physical development despite years passing by

- A particular case with the elderitch is that they can reform to the appeareance of an child when not enough "black blood" is given, these are cases when the dark sauna malfunctions.
theres two ways the elderitch combat this:

  • They let the elderitch grow over time by eating other living beings
  • They put them in the black sauna again hoping that it does not malfuntion again
